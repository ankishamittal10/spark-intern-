How It Works?

  • Step 01:
    Kickstart Your Journey
    Begin your journey with Spark Intern by enrolling in the program. Pay the minimal registration fee to secure your spot and access all program resources.
  • Step 02:
    Receive Your Welcome Letter
    After successful enrollment, receive your offer letter, program access details, and a comprehensive welcome kit via email to prepare for your internship journey.
  • Step 03:
    Expert-Led Learning
    Dive into a self-paced learning experience guided by industry mentors, including professionals from leading companies like MAANG. Access rich, practical resources designed for hands-on learning.
  • Step 04:
    Create Real-Impact Projects
    Apply your skills to real-world challenges by working on impactful projects. Build a portfolio showcasing your expertise to future employers and collaborators.
  • Step 05:
    Earn Your Internship Credentials
    Submit your completed projects and assessments to earn a Verified Internship Certificate and a Letter of Recommendation, showcasing your accomplishments to the industry.
